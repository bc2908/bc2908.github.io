Why iPhone X

Infrared Camera

An infrared camera reads the dot pattern,then sends the data to the Secure Enclave in the A11 Bionic chip to confirm a match.

True Tone

True Tone technology uses an advanced six‑channel ambient light sensor to subtly adjust the color temperature of the light.

Intuitive Gestures

Familiar gestures make navigation natural and intuitive. Instead of pressing a button, a single swipe takes you home from anywhere.

Front Camera

A tiny space houses some of the most sophisticated technology, including the cameras and sensors that enable Face ID.

Super Retina Display

With iPhone X, the device is the display. An all‑new 5.8‑inch Super Retina screen fills the hand and dazzles the eyes.1.

OLED Designed

The first OLED screen that rises to the standards of iPhone, with stunning colors, high brightness, and a 1,000,000 to 1 contrast ratio.

Wirless Connectivity

With no charging cable required, iPhone X is truly designed for the future of wireless.4 Charge with wireless charging stations and mats in hotels, cafes, and airports around the world.

Wireless charging for a wireless world. Charge with wireless charging stations and mats in hotels, cafes, and airports around the world.Introducing the AirPower mat. Just set your iPhone, Apple Watch, and AirPods anywhere on the mat to charge them wirelessly.

Your Face Is Your Secure Password

We Care about your privacy and security. With Face ID, iPhone X unlocks only when you’re looking at it. It’s also designed to prevent spoofing by photos or masks. Your Face ID information is encrypted and protected by the Secure Enclave. And it’s private — your data doesn’t leave your device and is never backed up to iCloud or anywhere else. .

For additional security, Face ID is attention aware, meaning it unlocks your iPhone X only when you look toward the device with your eyes open. That means Face ID can also reveal notifications and messages, keep the screen lit when you’re reading, or lower the volume of an alarm or ringer.
